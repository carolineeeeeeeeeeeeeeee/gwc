Cupcakes

Cupcakes are a delicious treat and simple to make. You can customize cupcakes by using different bases and adding candy, sprinkles, frosting, and a lot of other toppings.

The Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

  • 1 1/4 teaspoons baking powder

  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

  • 1 stick unsalted butter, softened

  • 1 cup sugar

  • 2 large eggs- room temperature

  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

  • 2/3 cup whole milk- room temperature

  • Frosting, recipe follows, for topping

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Line 12-cup muffin pan with paper liners.
  3. Whisk flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  4. Beat butter and sugar in a stand mixture with paddle attachment on medium-high speed until fluffy- about 4 minutes.
  5. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, scraping down bowl if needed.
  6. Beat in vanilla and reduce mixer speed to medium low, beat n half of flour mixture,then all of milk.
  7. Beat in the rest of the ingredients.
